Volunteering

Cool girls
Cool Girls is a non-profit organization dedicated to the self-empowerment of at-risk elementary and middle school aged girls in Atlanta, GA. Each summer my church, St. Peter & St. Paul Episcopal Church, provides a “Back to School” event for these girls. On this day, our church turns into a mini-mall for the girls to come and “shop” for back-to-school items such as backpacks, school supplies, and other personal necessities that have been donated by church members and area schools. The girls also participate in dancing, face-painting, and a day of encouragement and fun while in the safety of our church. This day prepares the girls with the needed supplies and items to help them succeed in the upcoming school year.

Flower Guild
The Flower Guild’s mission is glorifying God by enhancing the worship services with beautiful flowers. The Flower Guild arranges the altar flowers and any other special flowers in the church for our liturgies.

Golden Retriever Rescue of Atlanta
The Golden Retriver Rescue of Atlanta is a nonprofit organization solely run though dedicated volunteers. Their mission is to save Golden and Labrador Retrievers and find them forever homes.

MUST Ministries Food Pantry
MUST, a volunteer-driven organization, addresses the basic needs of individuals, families and children of Cobb County. Not only do they serve the community through their Summer Lunch Program, but they also have a warehouse run solely on donations. There, people can pick up needed necessities such as clothes, food, hygiene kits, and more.

High School Outreach

Cooking Crew
Students gather together to prepare, cook and serve meals for the Loaves and Fishes Community Kitchen at MUST Ministries in Marietta, GA.

Junior Achievement
This program works with elementary aged students to help them understand basic economics and to teach them about starting their own business, financial risk and reward, and problem-solving skills.

Monday in the Commons
Students gathered together to make packages/goody bags/survival kits for a variety of groups including elementary students, soldiers, and the less fortunate. We also made sandwiches for MUST Ministries, create letters and cards for soldiers, and braid together old tshirts to make dog toys.

Project Mail Call
The Project Mail Call organization provides a direct and personal method to send customized care-boxes to U.S. military serving overseas. At care-box packing sessions, volunteers pack boxes for military units based on the units unique wants and needs.

Splash of Color
A group that puts a creative spin on giving back to the community. At each meeting, members paint canvases to donate to homeless shelters throughout metro Atlanta.

Walton Community Outreach
Walton Community Outreach is a student-led, PTSA-sponsored service organization that supports the community through a variety of ongoing projects and programs. Various projects include playing bingo with the elderly, tutoring elementary kids, attending After School Program parties, preparing meals for the less fortunate, creating care packages for soldiers, and so much more!

Hobbies and Clubs

Best Friends Pet Care
Best Friends Pet Care is a state-of-the-art facility that provides pet boarding, grooming, and day camp services.

CHAARG
A fitness group that encourages working out and good health. From small groups to weekly workouts, it's a close-knit group of women that all have similar motivations to succeed not only in their studies, but also in their personal fitness journeys.

Ultimate Frisbee
A sport that focuses on teamwork, communication, and agility. However, this sport is more than just a way to hold a friendly competition, it's about developing character and sportmanship. It's about forming a community with those around you and respecting your opponents.
